FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoFlow

Is AutoFlow customer-facing?

No. AutoFlow is exclusively an internal operations tool for garage staff. Customers do not have access to the system, cannot create accounts, and cannot book appointments themselves. All bookings are created by your staff members.

Do customers book appointments through AutoFlow?

No. AutoFlow is a staff-only system. Customers contact your garage through phone, email, or walk-in, and your staff creates bookings in the system. This gives you complete control over scheduling and service delivery.

Can we customize services and pricing?

Yes. Administrators have full control over the service catalog. You can add, edit, or remove services, set pricing, define service durations, and organize services into categories. This ensures consistent pricing across all staff members.

How does AI-assisted booking work?

When creating a booking, AutoFlow analyzes the selected services and suggests optimal time slots based on service duration and technician availability. It also recommends the best-matched technician based on their specializations and current workload. Staff can accept or override these suggestions.

Can we export invoices and reports?

Yes. AutoFlow supports PDF export for invoices and various report formats. You can export individual invoices for customer records or bulk export reports for accounting purposes. Additional export formats and advanced reporting are on the roadmap.

What about roles and permissions?

AutoFlow has two primary roles: Admin and Staff. Admins have full access to all features including user management, service catalog, settings, and system configuration. Staff members have access to day-to-day operations like creating bookings, viewing schedules, and generating invoices. Admins control who has access to what.

How fast can we onboard?

Most garages are fully operational within 1-3 days. The process is simple: 1) Admin sets up services and pricing (1-2 hours), 2) Admin creates staff accounts (15 minutes), 3) Brief staff training (30 minutes per person), 4) Start creating bookings. We provide guided setup and optional onboarding assistance.

Can this be self-hosted or deployed on-premises?

Self-hosted and on-premises deployment options are on our roadmap. Currently, AutoFlow is offered as a cloud-hosted solution with enterprise-grade security. For organizations requiring on-premises deployment, please contact our sales team to discuss custom deployment options and timeline.
